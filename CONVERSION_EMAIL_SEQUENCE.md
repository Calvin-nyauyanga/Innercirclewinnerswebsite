## Optimization Strategy

### 1. Personalization Logic
*   **{{first_name}}**: Essential for open rates.
*   **{{target_result}}**: Dynamic based on industry (e.g., "more sales" for E-comm, "more leads" for Agencies).
*   **{{use_case}}**: Tailored to the vertical they selected during signup.

### 2. CTA Urgency Modulation
*   **Early (Email 1-2)**: Low friction ("Log in", "Copy template").
*   **Mid (Email 3-4)**: Benefit-driven ("Start testing", "Join community").
*   **Late (Email 5)**: Scarcity-driven ("Claim discount", "Expires tomorrow").

### 3. Unsubscribe Strategy
*   **The "Respectful Exit"**: Include a clear unsubscribe link in every footer.
*   **The "Pause" Option**: On the unsubscribe page, offer a "Pause emails for 30 days" option instead of a hard opt-out.
*   **Feedback Loop**: Ask "Why are you leaving?" with simple radio buttons (Too many emails, Not relevant, Trial ended).
