**Goal**: Lift homepage conversion rate from baseline to +25% within 12 weeks.

---

## 2. Statistical Analysis Template

### Success Criteria
*   **Confidence Level**: 95% minimum.
*   **Statistical Power**: 80% minimum.
*   **Minimum Detectable Effect (MDE)**: 5% relative lift.

### Winner Announcement Checklist
- [ ] Sample size reached for both variants.
- [ ] Confidence level > 95% (p-value < 0.05).
- [ ] No external factors (e.g., holiday sales, broken tracking) skewed results.
- [ ] Post-test segmentation shows consistent lift across devices (Mobile vs. Desktop).
- [ ] Documented "Why it won" for future strategy.
